### ðŸ“œ Verbatim Instructions:
Today we are going to make a pretend store. Have them make a store with their favorite toys. The different age groups will need to have appropriate prices for their age group. Set up the store in advance.
Go to your store and figure out the following:
The sum (the answer when you add them) if you buy two of the objects.
The difference (the answer when you subtract)  between how much money you have and how much you will need if you buy those two items.
The product (the answer when you multiply) if you buy multiples of one item.
Find the quotient (the answer when you divide) to figure out how much each item cost if you spent 72 cents and you bought nine of them?
Write an equation to represent what you did that includes coefficients, factors  and terms.

### ðŸ’¡ ðŸš€ Mission Strategy: Step-by-Step
*(Use this guide to help you complete the instructions above)*
1. Set up a store with toys and price tags.
2. Pick two toys.
3. **Sum:** Add the prices.
4. **Difference:** Subtract the smaller price from the bigger one.
5. **Product:** What if you bought 5 of the same toy? Multiply price x 5.
6. **Quotient:** If you have 72 cents, how many of this toy can you buy? (72 / price).
7. Write a math sentence (equation) for what you just did.
