## ðŸ—ºï¸ 4. The Adventure (Activity)
### ðŸ“œ Verbatim Instructions:
You are going to need several pieces of scratch paper today, a pencil and two dice. Split your first piece of paper into half. Roll your two dice and multiply the two dice and write the product at the top of one side of the paper, for example if you roll a two and a four - write 8. Roll your dice again and write the product on the top of the other side of the paper. You are going to find the greatest common factor of these two numbers. To find the factors you are looking for what numbers can be multiplied together to get that number. You can do this by gathering that number of items and splitting them into even piles and writing down the number in each pile. For example if your number was 12. You could make 1 pile of 12, 2 piles or 6, 3 piles of 4 - so you would write the factors as 1,2,3,4,6,12. Do the same thing with your second number. For example if it is 18, the factors would be 1,2,3,6,9,18.  To find the greatest common factor, you circle the biggest number that the two sides have in common. In this case it is 6. Repeat the activity several times.

### ðŸ’¡ ðŸš€ Mission Strategy: Step-by-Step
*(Use this guide to help you complete the instructions above)*
1. Take a piece of paper and draw a line down the middle.
2. **Left Side:** Roll your two dice. Multiply the numbers together (e.g., 2 x 4 = 8). Write that number at the top.
3. **Right Side:** Roll again. Multiply them. Write that number at the top.
4. **Find Factors:** Use your 80 items. If your number is 12, count 12 items. Can you split them into 2 equal piles? 3? 4? 5? If yes, write down the number of piles.
5. List all the factors for both numbers.
6. Circle the numbers that appear on **BOTH** lists. The biggest one is the Winner (GCF)!
