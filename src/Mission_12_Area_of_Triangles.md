### ðŸ’¡ ðŸš€ Mission Strategy: Step-by-Step
*(Use this guide to help you complete the instructions above)*
1. Draw a rectangle on graph paper. Count the squares to find Area (Base x Height).
2. Cut the rectangle in half diagonally.
3. Look! You have two triangles. That's why Triangle Area = 1/2 Base x Height.
