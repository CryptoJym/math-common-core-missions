### ðŸ’¡ ðŸš€ Mission Strategy: Step-by-Step
*(Use this guide to help you complete the instructions above)*
1. Roll a die 5 times. Write the numbers (e.g., 2, 5, 4, 7, 2).
2. **Sum:** Add them up (2+5+4+7+2 = 20).
3. **Count:** You rolled 5 times.
4. **Average:** Divide Sum by Count (20 / 5 = 4).
5. Repeat with more rolls!
